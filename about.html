About Kisoro TV

The Voice of South-Western Uganda

Who We Are

Kisoro TV is a digital media platform dedicated to showcasing the beauty, culture, news, and development stories of Kisoro District in South-Western Uganda. We serve as a bridge connecting local communities with the wider world.

Founded with a passion for storytelling and community development, we bring you authentic content about the stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, wildlife conservation, and the vibrant communities that call Kisoro home.

Our Mission

To inform, educate, and entertain while promoting tourism, preserving culture, and fostering community development in Kisoro District.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital media platform that showcases Kisoro District as a premier destination for tourism, culture, and sustainable development.

What We Do

We produce high-quality video content, news reports, documentaries, and cultural features that highlight:

  • Local news and community developments
  • Tourism opportunities including gorilla trekking and volcano hiking
  • Cultural heritage of Bafumbira, Bakiga, and Batwa communities
  • Environmental conservation efforts
  • Educational and social development initiatives

Our Values

We are committed to accuracy, integrity, and respect in all our reporting. We believe in the power of storytelling to inspire positive change, preserve cultural heritage, and promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities.
