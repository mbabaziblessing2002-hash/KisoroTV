Explore Kisoro

Discover breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and adventure

Mgahinga Gorilla National Park

Home to the endangered mountain gorillas, Mgahinga offers one of the most incredible wildlife experiences in Africa. Trek through dense bamboo forests to encounter these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

  • Gorilla trekking permits available
  • Golden monkey tracking
  • Guided nature walks

Virunga Volcanoes

Climb the majestic Virunga volcanoes - Mount Muhabura, Mount Gahinga, and Mount Sabyinyo. These volcanic peaks offer challenging hikes with spectacular views of the surrounding landscapes.

  • Mount Muhabura (4,127m)
  • Mount Sabyinyo (3,634m)
  • Mount Gahinga (3,474m)

Lake Mutanda

Known as the "Place of Islands," Lake Mutanda offers serene waters surrounded by stunning volcanic scenery. Perfect for canoeing, fishing, and enjoying breathtaking sunsets.

  • Canoeing and boat rides
  • Island hopping
  • Community visits
