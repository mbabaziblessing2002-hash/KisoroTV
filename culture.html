Our Rich Culture

Celebrating the heritage of Bafumbira, Bakiga, and Batwa

Cultural Communities

Bafumbira

The Bafumbira people are known for their rich agricultural traditions, vibrant dances, and unique cultural practices. Their territory spans the fertile slopes of the Virunga volcanoes.

Batwa

The Batwa are the indigenous forest people with a deep connection to the land. Their traditional knowledge of the forest and unique cultural heritage is an invaluable part of Kisoro's identity.

Bakiga

The Bakiga people are known for their terraced farming, strong community bonds, and vibrant cultural festivals. Their traditions reflect resilience and adaptation to the highland environment.

Cultural Festivals

Experience the vibrant cultural festivals that celebrate the heritage and traditions of Kisoro District. From the annual Abakyera Festival to traditional dance performances, there's always something to experience.

  • Traditional dance and music
  • Cultural storytelling
  • Traditional crafts and art
  • Local cuisine experiences
